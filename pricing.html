FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and packages.

What is included in the rental price?
All packages include the vessel, fuel, safety equipment, and basic insurance. Premium and VIP packages add crew, catering, and additional amenities as listed.
Can I extend my rental duration?
Yes, you can extend your rental subject to vessel availability. Additional hours are charged at 75% of the hourly rate. Contact our team for extensions.
Is there a refundable deposit required?
A refundable security deposit is required for all rentals. The amount varies by package: $500 for Basic, $1,000 for Premium, and $2,000 for VIP packages.
What happens in case of bad weather?
Your safety is our priority. If weather conditions are unsafe, we will reschedule your booking at no additional cost or provide a full refund.
Do you offer custom packages?
Absolutely! We specialize in creating bespoke experiences. Contact our concierge team to design a custom package tailored to your preferences.
