FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your yacht experience.

How do I book a yacht?
Simply browse our fleet, select your preferred vessel, choose your date and duration, and submit a booking request. Our team will confirm within 2 hours.
Is a captain included?
Yes, all our rentals include a licensed captain and crew. Premium and VIP packages also include additional crew members for catering and service.
Can I cancel my booking?
Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before departure. A 50% refund is offered for cancellations between 24-48 hours. No refunds within 24 hours.
What safety measures are in place?
All vessels are equipped with life jackets, GPS tracking, first aid kits, and emergency communication systems. Our crew is trained in advanced safety procedures.
What is the maximum guest capacity?
Capacity varies by vessel, ranging from 6 guests on speed boats to 30 guests on party yachts. VIP yachts can accommodate up to 20 guests for full-day charters.
Are food and beverages included?
Basic packages include water and soft drinks. Premium packages include a full catering menu. VIP packages feature gourmet dining with a private chef.
