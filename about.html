Our Story

Setting Sail Since 2015

Stackly was born from a passion for the sea and a vision to redefine luxury travel. Founded in 2015 by a team of maritime enthusiasts and hospitality experts, we have grown from a small fleet of three yachts to one of the most prestigious yacht rental companies in the world.

Every journey with Stackly is crafted with meticulous attention to detail. From the moment you step aboard, our dedicated crew ensures that your experience exceeds every expectation. We believe that the ocean has the power to transform, inspire, and create memories that last a lifetime.

Mission: To deliver unparalleled sea experiences
Vision: To be the global benchmark in luxury yachting
Safety: ISO certified with zero-compromise standards
